What the Lounge Is

The Flavor Lab is for deep analysis. The Scout Report is for intel. The Lounge is for everything else — the culture, the community, and the reality that bourbon is more fun when you're sharing it with people you like.

Content here tends to be more personal, more opinionated, and a little less structured. That's on purpose.
