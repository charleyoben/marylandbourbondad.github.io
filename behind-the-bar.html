The Maryland Old Fashioned

Classic Build · Stirred · Strong

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Sagamore Spirit Straight Rye
  • ½ tsp Demerara sugar syrup (or 1 sugar cube)
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash orange bitters
  • Large ice cube
  • Orange peel and brandied cherry to garnish

Method

  1. Combine rye, sugar syrup, and both bitters in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir for 30 seconds — you want dilution but not too much.
  3. Strain over a large ice cube in a rocks glass.
  4. Express an orange peel over the glass, run it around the rim, and drop it in.
  5. Add a brandied cherry if you're feeling generous.
MBD Note: Using a local Maryland rye here is the whole point. Sagamore's mineral quality and high-rye mash bill were practically made for this format.

Bourbon Smash

Summer Cocktail · Shaken · Refreshing

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Elijah Craig Small Batch
  • ¾ oz fresh lemon juice
  • ¾ oz simple syrup
  • 6 fresh mint leaves
  • Splash of club soda
  • Mint sprig to garnish

Method

  1. Muddle mint leaves gently in a shaker — don't overdo it or it goes bitter.
  2. Add bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and ice. Shake hard for 10 seconds.
  3. Double-strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
  4. Top with a small splash of club soda and garnish with a mint sprig.
MBD Note: This is the backyard party workhorse. Easy to batch, crowd-pleasing, and it uses bourbon people actually own.
