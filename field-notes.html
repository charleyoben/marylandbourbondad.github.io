Old Line Spirits — Tasting Room Visit

📍 Baltimore, MD

Finally made it back to Old Line's tasting room in Baltimore. The American Single Malt is still the standout — more complex than it gets credit for, with a malt character that leans closer to Scotch than bourbon. The rum lineup has improved significantly. Worth the drive from Columbia.

Apr 2026

Total Wine Haul — Ellicott City

📍 Ellicott City, MD

Found Booker's 2025-02 at MSRP — grabbed two. Also picked up the new Angel's Envy Triple Oak which had been eluding me. Total Wine in EC consistently has better allocation than the Columbia locations. Pro tip: go Thursday morning.

Mar 2026

Sagamore Spirit Distillery

📍 Port Covington, Baltimore, MD

Brought a friend who'd never done a distillery tour before. Sagamore's tour is one of the better-produced experiences in the state. The rickhouse is legitimately impressive, and the guide actually knew the answers to real questions. The tasting room pour of the cask strength was exceptional. Better than I remembered.
