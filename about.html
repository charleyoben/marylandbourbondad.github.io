Who Is the Maryland Bourbon Dad?

I'm a Maryland-based bourbon enthusiast who got tired of the pretension. Too many bourbon sites exist to tell you what you should like, what you must have tried, and which bottles are beneath your attention.

That's not what I'm doing here.

I'm a dad, a husband, a professional who squeezes bourbon into real life — at backyard gatherings, on grocery store shelves at 7pm on a Tuesday, and during the occasional honest-to-goodness distillery trip.

I take notes. I score bottles. I share what I find. No gatekeeping, no hype, no unicorn chasing.

"No snobbery. Just honest taste."

Maryland is a genuinely interesting bourbon state — Old Line Spirits, Sagamore Spirit, and a growing craft scene that doesn't get nearly enough attention. I cover that, plus everything that takes me beyond state lines.
