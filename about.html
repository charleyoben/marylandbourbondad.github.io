The Story

Maryland Roots. Bourbon Soul.

Who is the Maryland Bourbon Dad?

"I started this brand because I noticed a gap. People were chasing unicorns, but they weren't enjoying the journey. I wanted to build a community for the Marylander who loves a good pour as much as a crab feast."

MBD is about more than just finding the rarest bottles. It's about the science of the blend, the history of the distillery, and the people you share the glass with.

Seek. Sip. Savor the Moment.
