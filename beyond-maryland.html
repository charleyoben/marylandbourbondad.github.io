Planning a Kentucky Run?

MBD's recommended two-trip strategy: Trip 1 — Louisville Whiskey Row (Evan Williams, Angel's Envy, Old Forester, Peerless — all walkable in a day). Trip 2 — Bourbon Country (Buffalo Trace, Woodford Reserve, Willett, Bardstown Bourbon Company, Jim Beam — two days minimum, base yourself in Bardstown).

Never drink and drive. Designate a driver or book a tour service.
